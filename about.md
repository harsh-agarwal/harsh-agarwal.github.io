## The Journey So Far

I started my story in **Varanasi** — a city where, as they say, people come to find eternity. There's something about living in Kashi that quietly teaches you to think bigger than the ordinary. I graduated from **IIT BHU Varanasi in 2018** with a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering, and those years remain some of the most formative of my life.

From there, I headed to **Carnegie Mellon University** to pursue a Master's in Robotics under **Prof. Kris Kitani**, diving deep into computer vision, multi-modal localization, and building systems that help machines understand the world the way we do.

But the itch to *build* something from scratch never really went away.

---

## Building Rephrase AI → Adobe

In **2020**, along with **Ashray, Nisheeth, and Shivam**, I co-founded **[Rephrase AI](https://www.rephrase.ai)** — back when text-to-video was more of a research moonshot than a product category. We dove headfirst into synthetic media and generative video, building some of the most compelling AI-driven video experiences the world had seen at the time.

We cloned and created digital avatars of **100+ celebrities** — Virat Kohli, Rohit Sharma, Shah Rukh Khan, Hrithik Roshan, and many more. But the moment that truly put us on the map? A **Cadbury ad featuring a digitally recreated SRK** — which went on to win the **[Cannes Lions Titanium Award](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5WECsbqAQSk)**. That one still gives me chills.

**Rephrase AI was acquired by Adobe**, and that opened an entirely new chapter.

---

## Today — Adobe Firefly & the Future of Video

I currently lead **Applied Science and Machine Learning (India)** at Adobe as a **Senior Manager**, working on **[Adobe Firefly's](https://www.adobe.com/products/firefly.html) text-to-video pipelines** for professional and next-generation video creators.

The mission is something I genuinely believe in: *making movie creation seamless, and unlocking human imagination at scale.* Whether you're a seasoned filmmaker or someone with a story but no studio — I want the tools we build to get out of your way and let you create.

My research interests have grown with the times. What started as a fascination with **Computer Vision and perception** has expanded into everything that large foundational models can do — and more importantly, what they *could* do. I'm in a continuous hunt to explore what's possible when you combine these models with real-world systems that actually ship.

I think of myself as a **generalist at heart** (Rephrase taught me that!). I love to code, I love to build, and I care deeply about creating systems that genuinely add value to people's lives.
